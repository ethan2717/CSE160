Minecraft-esque Scavenger Hunt

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

MS: _ // FPS: _

Instructions:

  • You are in a maze, surrounded by walls of lava.
  • There are three different "biomes" represented by sand, stone, and wood, respectively.
  • Each biome has one stash of diamond ore and one stash of TNT.
  • Try to see if you can find all six stashes!
  • Bonus: observe the sky transition from day to night as you travel through the maze!

Controls:

Key Function
W move forward
A move left
S move backward
D move right
Q pan left
E pan right
